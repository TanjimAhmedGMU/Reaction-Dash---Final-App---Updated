Reaction Dash

Welcome to Reaction Dash!

  • After starting the game, the screen will change color after a random delay.
  • Click as fast as you can when the color changes.
  • You will receive scores based on how fast you react.
  • Clicking too early will result in a -40 score.
  • You will play 8 rounds and get your total score.
  • You'll get stats like your fastest, slowest, and average reaction time.
  • Press Start Game when you're ready to begin!
